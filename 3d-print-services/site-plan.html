Overview

Purpose

Our custom 3D printing service brings your ideas to life by collaborating with clients to design and create high-quality, personalized 3D-printed objects. We simplify the process, offering expert support from concept to final product.

Audience

The website targets beginners, hobbyists, and small business owners new to 3D printing, who want to explore custom designs or prototypes but need an easy-to-use platform to request prints or browse past work for inspiration. It’s designed for users with limited technical knowledge, emphasizing clear navigation and straightforward request processes.

Content

Home page

My website’s about custom 3D printing services where people send me ideas or designs, and I print them. It’s for stuff like figurines or phone stands. It’s easy and doesn’t cost a ton. People will visit because I make it simple and work with them to get it right. It saves time since they don’t need their own printer. My site’s different because I’m just one guy, so it’s personal and affordable.

Images for the Home page

Gallery

This page shows pictures of stuff I’ve printed, like a D&D miniature or a phone holder. Each has a note about what the client wanted. It’ll get people excited about what they could make. I want it to inspire them to think of their own ideas, like a custom coaster or lamp.

Images for the Page 2

Contact Me

This page explains how to order. You fill out a form with your idea or design file. If you don’t have a file, I can help with a sketch. We pick materials and colors, I give a price, and then I print it. It answers stuff like “What do I need?” or “How long’s it take?” It’s super easy, and I’m here to help if you’re confused.
